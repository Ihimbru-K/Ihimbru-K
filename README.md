## Selected Projects

### Authentikate `live pilot`
Biometric exam attendance system replacing paper sign-in sheets in universities. Fingerprint verification, real-time tracking, role-based admin — built for scale, not demos.

> Pilot running at the University of Bamenda · ~30,000 students at full approval

`Flutter` `FastAPI` `PostgreSQL` `Firebase` `JWT`

---

### SheyDoc `in development`
Telemedicine platform built for sub-Saharan Africa. Video consultations, medical records, FCM notifications — architected for low-bandwidth conditions where dropped connections are the norm.

> Targeting Cameroon's mental health access gap · 4M+ underserved population

`Flutter` `FastAPI` `Stream Video SDK` `Appwrite` `Firebase`

---

### Propti — TRMS `in development`
Tenant and rent management platform for Cameroonian landlords. Rent cycles, partial payments, in-app tenancy agreements with digital signing, and PDF generation — targeting a market that still runs on WhatsApp and handshake deals.

> Focus: Bamenda property market · Freemium + per-agreement monetization model

`Flutter` `FastAPI` `PostgreSQL` `Firebase`
